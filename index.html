About the Project

The goal of this project is to convert a series of interactive astronomy simulations from Flash to HTML5 so that they do not become obsolete. Originally created by Kevin Lee at the University of Nebraska) and continued by David Helfand, Professor of Astronomy and Department Chair at Columbia University, they will be working closely with CTL to make the conversions. Currently there are 8 simulations that will need to be converted, with Nik Nyby (Developer) taking the lead in the development. However, there are are over 100 of these simulations that may need to eventually be converted, so part of the conversion process will be dedicated to creating a framework for the conversions that can be used repeatedly.
